Vector3f SquareToSphereCapUniform(const Vector2f &sample, Float thetaMin)¶

Uniformly sample a vector on a spherical cap around (0, 0, 1)

A spherical cap is the subset of a unit sphere whose directions make an angle of less than ‘theta’ with the north pole. This function expects the minimum angle ‘theta’ as a parameter.
